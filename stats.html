Crutches that was used to define points for each song

Strength — damage, impact.
Strength: 1-2 – very weak and light, 3-4 – gentle, 5-6 – you can feel it, 7-8 – impactful, 9-10 – powerful, aggressive, 11-12 – long-lasting
Dexterity — tempo and also character’s speed.
Dexterity: 1-2 – too fast or too slow, annoying, 3-4 – monotonous, not interesting, 5-6 – medium, decent, 7-8 – expressive, harmonic with the lyrics, 9-10 – changing, funny tempo, 11-12 – extraordinary
Vitality — healing property of a song and also hp of a character.
Vitality: 1-2 – drains life from you, 3-4 – unpleasant to listen to, 5-6 – not affecting hp/mid character, 7-8 – improves mood, supports, 9-10 – have a healing effect, 11-12 – resurrects
Energy — mana, dreaminess, otherworldliness, vibe.
Energy: 1-2 – not charismatic, dry, 3-4 – basic, 5-6 – at this point you can feel it, 7-8 – makes you think of it, 9-10 – makes you forget your problems, 11-12 – stuns, makes you forget yourself for a moment
